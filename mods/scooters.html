This is a mod that adds two-wheeled stand-up scooters to the game!

For more information at this time, check the design decisions page.

Additional Information

Regarding Quilt: Scooters intends to support Quilt and Fabric, and rely on neither. Scooters before release was developed on Fabric, and will add Quilt support after 1.0.0.

Requirements

  • Fabric Loader or compatible
  • Fabric API or comaptible
