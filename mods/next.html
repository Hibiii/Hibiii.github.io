This is not an actual mod. This page only contains ideas for a future mod, which I haven't started work on.

Aesthetics

These ideas are 100% compatible with, well, all servers, and should not in theory give you an advantage.

Particle Effects

  • Water dripping from ice when and where it can melt
  • Variable smoke from fires (less in hot places, more in cold places)
  • Souls escaping soul sand at random
  • Sparks coming from complete beacon pyramids
  • Pollen from flowers
  • Item despawning
  • Players logging in and out

Sound Effects

  • Water flowing noises from rivers and oceans (large bodies still water)
  • Chain clanking from hung chains
  • Water rustling under boats
  • Amethyst randomly chiming
  • Scaffolding creaking
  • Clocks on item frames ticking like wall clocks
  • Electrical noises from redstone components
  • Metallic creaking from extended pistons
  • Coil whine from active comparators, louder if in subtract mode
  • Wind noise all the time

Gameplay Additions

These ideas, while they may be 100% compatible with the other side of the client-server paradigm, is just, different. Any clientside mods aren't guaranteed to not trip anticheats, but are less likely.

Clientside Effects

  • Heat Stroke
    • Caused by sun exposure, hot biomes, and merely existing in The Nether, as well as being on fire(1)
    • Symptoms are slowness, nausea, weakness and mining fatigue
    • Treated by water bottles, fire resistance, and items that provide shade(2)

    1: Technically, heat stroke would only be inflicted upon being extinguished because adrenaline of being on fire and all. 2: Items that provide shade are to be set with a resource pack. In one of my resource packs, the Totem of Undying is an umbrella uwu.

  • Cold
    • Caused by swimming in cold biomes, being too high in the world, outdoors at night in cold biomes, as well as deep, dark, and narrow caves, and receiving rain without a shade
    • Symptom is pretty much the freezing effect, but no actual hurting
    • Treated by standing next to fire, next to insulation (wool) and wearing leather armor

Serverside: additional random ticks

    Aging of Building Materials

  • Mossy cobblestone or stone bricks turning into normal ones in sunlight or in heat
  • Cobblestone and stone bricks turning into mossy when wet
  • Bricks and tiles crack under pressure and heat

    • Nature Finds a Way

  • Moss carpet spreads in dark and humid conditions
  • Moss turns into pozdol under sunlight
  • Vines growing under leaves
  • Azalea leaves bloom and wilt
  • Leaves can spread
  • Rarely, trees can create grass

    • Gardens and Farms

  • Flowers on farmland spread to other farmland
  • Oak leaves drop apples
  • Grass can grow grass blades that can turn into tall grass
  • Tall grass can wither into small grass, which themselves can also disappear
  • Grass can die in dry biomes

    • Settlements

  • Lichen can spread where dripstone drips water (requires copper ore)
  • Gravel settles down into dirt creating coarse dirt
  • Gravel settles into dirt around in large bodies of water and with a larger range
  • Dirt can turn into mud in large bodies of water
  • Mud deposits dirt onto nearby coarse dirt, in large bodies of water

    • Into the Nether

  • Mushrooms spread faster on nylium
  • Weeping Vines spread onto Netherrack, and rarely, Nether Wart blocks
  • Twisting Vines spread, rarely, onto Warped Nylium and Warped Wart blocks
  • Fire Coral does not die in The Nether
