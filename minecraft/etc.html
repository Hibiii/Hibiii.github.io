Resource Packs

Mojang does not advertise the monster that are resource packs, they already have a lot of functionality and I only cheer for more. You can animate nearly anything, nearly anything. connected textures are also possible, also randomized blocks. You can also change where on a player an item will show, it's bonkers!

Examples of what can be done

  • Bushier tree leaves (maybe even non-cubic), animated texture for the wind, as well as randomized blocks;
  • Twisting vines that slowly twist themselves, chains that sway a little with the wind (previously with texture animations but now with vertex shaders);
  • Chorus plant log with small flowers (randomized) as well as "weeping" chorus flowers with vines protuding out of bounding box;
  • Fixed stair and double slab textures;
  • Redstone wire resembles actual wire with insulation, 3D models, hoppers visibly connect to other blocks, smaller repeaters and comparators;
  • Flowers bloom and open as time goes by, saplings, grass, and wheat that wave with the wind;
  • "Dirty" concrete with randomized textures/blocks and dirty edges (when not connected to other blocks); slightly damage to tiles on blocks like cut sandstone, quartz bricks; variated damage on cracked blocks;
  • Customized tools and hats (tagged carved pumpkin);
  • Completely custom items without mods.
